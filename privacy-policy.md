# Privacy Policy for Emoji-nary

**Last Updated: January 11, 2026**

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Emoji-nary ("we", "our", or "the app") handles information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

**We do not collect any personal information.**

Emoji-nary is designed with your privacy in mind. The app:

- Does not require you to create an account
- Does not collect your name, email, or any contact information
- Does not track your location
- Does not access your contacts, photos, or other personal data

## Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores the following information locally on your device only:

- Your game progress (puzzles completed, coins earned)
- Your app preferences (such as dark mode settings)
- Your purchase status (if you purchased "Remove Ads")

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to us or any third party.

## Advertising

Emoji-nary displays optional rewarded video advertisements through Google AdMob. When you choose to watch an ad, Google's advertising service may collect certain information as described in Google's Privacy Policy (https://policies.google.com/privacy).

You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device's Settings under Privacy > Advertising.

You may purchase "Remove Ads" to eliminate all advertisements from the app.

## In-App Purchases

Emoji-nary offers an optional in-app purchase to remove advertisements. These transactions are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store any payment information.

## Children's Privacy

Emoji-nary does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. The app is safe for users of all ages.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above.
